Emanuela Struffolino

Sociologist

About

Since June 2021, I am Assistant Professor of Economic Sociology at the University of Milan, Department of Social and Political Sciences.

I am a member of the Executive Board of the SAA-Sequence Analysis Association since 2018. In January 2022, I joined the Editorial Team of the Journal of Family Research.

Between September 2020 and Novembre 2021 I was co-PI of the project KOMPAKK-Household structures and economic risks in East and West Germany during the COVID-19 pandemic: compensation or accumulation? hosted at at the Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin, Institute of Social Sciences.

Between October 2020 and March 2021, I was guest Professor of Social Policy (W2 Vertretungsprofessur) at the Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin. Between April and September 2020, I was guest Professor of Macrosociology (W3 Vertretungsprofessur) at the Institute of Sociology at the Freie Universität Berlin .

From 2015 to 2019 I was a postdoctoral fellow at research group “Demography and Inequality" Research Group and then at the the research unit “Skill Formation and Labor Markets" at the WZB Berlin Social Science Center.

I obtained my PhD in Sociology at the University of Milano-Bicocca and worked as research fellow at the Swiss National Centre for Competence in Research “LIVES - Overcoming vulnerability: A life-course perspective" at the University of Lausanne.

On this website, you can find information on my publications and ongoing research.
