Emanuela Struffolino

Social Scientist

About

I am guest Professor of Macrosociology (W3 Vertretungsprofessur) at the Institute of Sociology at the Freie Universität Berlin for the summer semester 2020 and long-term guest researcher at the research unit “Skill Formation and Labor Markets" at the WZB Berlin Social Science Center and at the chair for Microsociology at the Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin. Between 2015 and 2019 I was a postdoctoral fellow at research group “Demography and Inequality" Research Group at the WZB. I obtained my PhD in Sociology at the University of Milano-Bicocca and worked as research fellow at the Swiss National Centre for Competence in Research “LIVES - Overcoming vulnerability: A life-course perspective" at the University of Lausanne. My research interests include life-course sociology, gender inequalities in the labor market, social stratification dynamics, poverty, and methods for longitudinal data. I am a member of the Executive Board of the SAA-Sequence Analysis Association .

On this website, you can find information on my publications and ongoing research.
